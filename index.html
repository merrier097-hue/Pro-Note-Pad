About Sweet life Novels

Sweet life Novels is a simple, powerful reading space created by Tallen. It’s built for real stories: funny, sad, romantic, motivational, psychological, and everything in between.

Here, you just open and read—no sign up, no login. Stories are stored safely in the cloud so they stay available for you and everyone who visits.

Tallen believes in storytelling as a way to heal, laugh, learn, and grow. Every card you open is a piece of life, written and curated with care.

Built with love, discipline, and a little bit of stubbornness.
